The Adjustable Modular Shelving System

Pairing industrial and Scandinavian aesthetics, the Forma wall shelf strikes a beautiful balance between these two design movements. Composed of wood with steel accents, the Forma adds interest to a space, providing the perfect backdrop to your art, books and treasured objects. The space between shelves can be adjusted allowing for endless arrangements. Each part is sold separately and is completely modular.
